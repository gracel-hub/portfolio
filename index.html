About

I'm a career advisor and educator at Columbia University's MS Climate Finance program, working with students from backgrounds spanning investment banking, environmental science, engineering, and policy.

My path here wasn't linear. I spent over a decade in capital markets and fintech — at BNP Paribas managing institutional client relationships across fixed income derivatives, then at Wealthfront helping scale operations from $2B to $20B AUM. I then earned a Master's in Sustainability Management from Columbia and spent several years consulting on ESG strategy, GHG accounting, and biodiversity risk for organizations including ING Group and early-stage climate startups.

That combination — deep finance fluency plus hands-on climate work — is what I bring to students navigating a field that demands both.
